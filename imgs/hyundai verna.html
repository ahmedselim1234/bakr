News Learn about the specifications and prices of the new Hyundai Verna Korean Hyundai unveiled the all-new generation of the Verna after a long wait, and it is one of the most widespread cars on the Egyptian street over the past 20 years. The new Hyundai Verna features two engine options, unprecedented standard safety factors, an attractive design in line with new Hyundai cars, and a luxurious cabin equipped with advanced technologies, with a price starting at 410,000 Egyptian pounds. the engine The Hyundai Verna relies on two engines, the first of which is a 1500 cc GDi gasoline engine, equipped with a turbocharger, with a power of 113 horsepower and a torque of 144 Newton metres, at 4500 rpm. It is connected to a 6-speed manual transmission, or a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (DCT). The second is a 1,500 cc MPi gasoline engine, with a power of 158 hp and 253 Nm of torque, at 3,500 rpm. It is connected to a 6-speed manual transmission and an intelligent variable automatic transmission (IVT). Safety factors Hyundai Verna's safety features include 6 airbags, for the driver, front passenger, and curtains, an anti-lock braking system (ABS), an electronic brake force distribution system (EBD), an electronic stability control system (ESC), emergency stop signals (ESS), and disc brakes. In addition to the front collision warning system, the front collision avoidance system with cars and pedestrians, the blind spot warning system, the lane keeping and lane departure warning system, and front and rear sensors. Hill Start Assist System (HAC), Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS), Hyundai SmartSense driver assistance system, and cruise control. Dimensions The length of the Hyundai Verna is 4535 mm, its width is 1765 mm, its height is 1475 mm, and its wheelbase is 2670 mm. the design The all-new Hyundai Verna comes with a distinctive exterior design with a curved roof, LED headlights, a black chrome parametric front grille, and attached LED taillights. With sporty 16-inch aluminum wheels, Z-shaped side stripes, chrome door handles, and a shark fin antenna. Cabin The cabin of the new Hyundai Verna is equipped with advanced technologies, most notably a 10.25-inch central screen to control information and luxury systems, and a digital instrument screen. It has air conditioning, a smart electric sunroof that operates with voice commands, leather seats with heating, 64-color ambient lighting, and a Bose Premium Sound system, consisting of 8 speakers. the prices The price of the all-new Hyundai Verna in India ranges from 1,089,900 to 1,737,900 Indian rupees, which is equivalent to 410,000 to 650,000 Egyptian pounds, according to prices.
